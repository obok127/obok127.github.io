Research in Progress

SV2U-CVR / RQ-SV2U-CVR

User-level conversion labels are often unavailable or unreliable in consented analytics environments. SV2U estimates user-equivalent period CVR from aggregate users, visits, and converted visits.

arXiv plannedGitHub repo plannedEnglish talk planned

Core estimator

V2U-CVR converts visit conversion probability into user-equivalent conversion probability using visits-per-user exposure.

p = 1 - (1 - C/V)V/U

SV2U applies the transform by segment. RQ-SV2U adds reliability weights for telemetry-risk environments.
